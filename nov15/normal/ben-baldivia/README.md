## Sudoku Solver

This program is a simple program to solve 9x9 sudoku boards

## Usage

Simply run `go run sudoku.go`

Enter the board in following example format:

    2 1 _ 3 _ _ _ _ _
    _ _ _ _ 6 _ _ 5 _
    _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
    3 _ _ _ _ _ 7 _ 2
    _ _ 4 _ 5 _ _ _ _
    _ _ _ _ _ _ 1 _ _
    _ _ _ 1 _ 2 _ 8 _
    _ 3 6 _ _ _ _ _ _
    _ _ _ 7 _ _ _ _ _
